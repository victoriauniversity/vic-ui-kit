Arrive and enroll

Welcome to Victoria. Your new life is underway—but there’s still plenty to do before you can start studying.

Talk to us about your study options

Our liaison officers provide objective advice to help you make the right decisions when planning your first year of study and can provide assistance with selecting your admission type when you apply to enrol.

Book a course planning appointment for one-to-one advice, email course-advice@vuw.ac.nz, or call 0800 VICTORIA (0800 842 867).
