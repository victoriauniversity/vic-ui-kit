The University is offering an on-campus experience for the remainder of 2020. In Trimester 2, normal face-to-face lectures will be offered for all undergraduate courses (unless the course coordinator is unable to return to Wellington). For every course, students can meet with academic staff and most courses will also offer additional in-person options such as tutorials, labs, workshops and placements. At the same time, all courses will also be available to be taken remotely (unless clearly marked otherwise in the course search).
