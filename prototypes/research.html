Study with us

Explore a topic that interests you, create new knowledge and become an expert in your field with a PhD, professional doctorate or masters by research.

We offer a research environment with highly experienced and engaged supervisors, extensive connections to industry, government and communities, and a focus on addressing global issues.

Learn more

Partner with us

At Victoria we’re driven by the impact our research has on the real world. We work directly with business and entrepreneurs to turn our world-class research into real solutions for you.

Companies across a diverse range of industries and communities have benefited from our research. Be an industry leader and let us help you make a difference.

Learn more

Engage our students

Victoria students shine in whatever capacity you engage with them. There are many ways you can take advantage of our talented students and graduates.

Discover the ways you can work with our students including, work placements, internships, short-term projects and student or graduate employment.

Learn more

Support for researchers

Victoria’s Research Office delivers professional research support services with functions spanning grants, development, ethics and integrity, policy, performance and reporting.

Our team works with academic staff and senior managers to address strategic priorities in research across the University. We also provide high-level strategic and administrative support for researchers at all levels and career stages.
